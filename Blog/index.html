first entry

12/20/18

I'm still not quite sure how I want to structure this blog ... I've been looking up a lot of other blogs and can't help but envy their designs. I don't want to copy them persay, but I want to create my own original blog composition by modelling off of other ones. For now, I will suffice with this!
So I am writing this at school, and today I only had one class. Now for the entire day I studied Japanese, ate food, and then studied coding. It's approximately 2:38pm, and I am surrounded by some really hard working people.
I wonder about whether or not they take any breaks. My teacher on my left showed me his payslip today, and said,"I make this much money, yet I work very hard!" and bursted into laughter with the teacher to my right.
Then we started to work silently away. Then shortly afterwards, one of my other teachers requested me to write a graduation message for the grade 6 students. I grabbed the last snowman piece of paper and wrote something along the lines of:


Hello wonderful grade 6 students. Congratulations! I hope you continue studying English, and every other subject with all your heart. Never stop chasing your dreams, and when life gives you lemons, make lemonade.


I thought long and hard about that metaphor today. I realized that I really did receive a lot of lemons in my life.
Shortly after, my teacher on my left showed me his message to the grade 6 students:

"The truth shall set you free."
